Home 14-day program — full reference

Bodyweight + jump rope. ~30–35 min. Equipment: jump rope, doorframe pull-up bar. Companion to the day-by-day pages.

The rules

  1. When: Morning. Alarm → bathroom → shorts → rope. No phone first. No coffee first. No decisions.
  2. Fallback: Lunch if morning is blocked.
  3. Duration: 30–35 minutes.
  4. Structure: Odd days = fixed workout. Even days = pick one play menu.
  5. Success: Did you show up? That is the metric.

Benchmark (days 1 & 14)

Do before the main session. ~3 minutes.

  1. Max push-ups in one set. Clean form, chest to floor, no long pause at top. Write the number down.
  2. 1-minute rope test. Timer on. Skip—when you trip, reset and continue. Count total good skips. Write it down.
  3. Dead hang. Overhand bar, feet off ground, hang. Time it. Write it down.

On day 14, compare to day 1. Do not peek at day 1 numbers until then.

Block 1 — Rope warm-up (5 min)

At ~10 skips max, continuous skipping is not the goal. Skill practice + cardio fill—rhythm, not lungs.

What Duration
Rope: skip until trip ~10–20s
March in place (high knees, no rope) ~15s
Rope: skip until trip ~10–20s
Jumping jacks ~15s
Rope: skip until trip ~10–20s
March in place ~15s

Repeat the cycle for 5 minutes. Stay moving; every trip is practice. By week 2, rope segments tend to lengthen naturally.

Block 2 — Circuit exercises

Five moves in order. Rest 90–120s after all five = one round.

# Exercise Week 1 reps Focus
1 Inchworm (no chest to floor) 3 Full body, shoulders, core
2 Push-ups 6 Chest, triceps, shoulders
3 Kick-throughs 4/side Core, shoulders, hips
4 Dead hang Max (aim 8–10s) Grip, shoulders, back
5 Sit-up with kick 6/side Core, hip flexors

Inchworm: Hands walk to plank, pause 1s, walk back, stand—no chest to floor yet. Worm sprawl (upgrade): add chest-to-floor and push-up at plank before walking back.

Week 2: pick one upgrade per session, not all: push-ups 6→8; inchworm→worm sprawl; kick-throughs 4→5/side; add a 3rd round; dead hang—hold longer than last time.

Block 3 — Finisher (5 min)

Default: Same idea as warm-up—rope attempts alternating with rope-free cardio for 5 minutes.

If stiff—mobility instead:

Time Move
0:00–0:45 Deep squat hold
0:45–1:30 Hip circles — left
1:30–2:15 Hip circles — right
2:15–3:00 Lying spine twist — left
3:00–3:45 Lying spine twist — right
3:45–5:00 Child’s pose or easy stretch

Play menu A — Rope skills

~30 min. More time on the rope = faster progress.

Block Time Focus
1 5 min Regular bounce. Trip, reset. Note best streak.
2 5 min Alternate foot (one foot at a time).
3 5 min 20s rope / 20s jacks, repeat.
4 5 min Slow singles—wrist flick, not big jumps.
5 5 min Free play, experiment.
6 5 min March in place + stretch.

If your streak passes ~20+, swap jack fill for longer continuous rope.

Play menu B — Legs & core

5 min rope warm-up (same as fixed days), then:

Exercise Sets × reps Rest
Bodyweight squats 3×12 45s
Half Cossack squats 3×4/side 45s
Reverse lunges 3×6/side 45s
Triangle crunches 3×8/side 30s
Plank hold 3×20s 30s

Then 5 min rope or mobility finisher. Week 2 ideas: squats → jump squats 3×6; half Cossack deeper; plank 20s→30s.

Play menu C — Push & pull

5 min rope warm-up, then:

Exercise Sets × reps Rest
Wide push-ups 3×5 60s
Dead hang 3× max 60s
Incline push-ups 3×8 45s
Dead hang 3× max 60s
Inchworms / worm sprawls 3×3 45s

Incline push-ups build volume with better form than narrow variants when strength is limited. Dead hangs build the base for future pulling work.

Play menu D — Movement

5 min rope warm-up, then:

Exercise Sets × work Rest
Bear crawl forward + back 3× ~3 m each way 30s
Kick-throughs 3×4/side 30s
Crab walk forward + back 3× ~3 m each way 30s
Deep squat hold + hip shifts 3×20s 20s
Bridge hold 3×15s 20s
Floor scorpion twist 3×4/side 20s

Then 5 min free mobility. Easiest menu—good when sore or low energy.

Pull-up bar progression

For the 14-day block, level 0 — dead hangs is enough. Advance only when comfortable.

Level Focus
0 — Dead hangs Hang as long as you can, 3 sets.
1 — Long hangs Aim 20–30s; optional shoulder shrugs.
2 — Flex hangs Chin over bar, hold.
3 — Negatives Lower slowly from top.
4 — Pull-ups Full reps, accumulate sets.

Missed a day

Situation Action
Missed 1 day Do today’s planned session. No doubling back.
Missed 2+ days Any play menu tomorrow, then resume the calendar where you left off.
Missed a week Do not restart from zero. Pick up with a play day; completed days still count.

Nutrition (personal targets)

Personal reference only—not medical advice. Adjust with a professional if you have health conditions.

Protein

  • 0.8 g per lb body weight, minimum 120 g/day.
  • At 94 kg (~207 lb): about 165 g protein per day.

Formula: max(120, bodyweight_lb × 0.8) grams per day.

Fat

  • 0.3 g per lb body weight.
  • At 94 kg: about 62 g fat per day.

Formula: bodyweight_lb × 0.3 grams per day.

Calories

Target roughly 10–20% below TDEE (total daily energy expenditure).

  • 10% deficit: daily calories ≈ TDEE × 0.90
  • 20% deficit: daily calories ≈ TDEE × 0.80

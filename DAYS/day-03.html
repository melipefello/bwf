Today’s workout

Do all push-up sets first, then all row sets (not alternating). Example flow: 7 push-ups, 60s rest, repeat ×4, then 5 rows, 60s rest, repeat ×4.

Exercise Sets × reps Rest
Push-ups 4×7 60s
Rows or reverse push-ups 4×5 60s
Intensity: If you are failing sets or near failure, you are going too hard for this phase—ease the variation or incline.

Reading

Row guide; equipment options or temporary reverse push-ups if you cannot row yet.
